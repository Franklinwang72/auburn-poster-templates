## Usage

Open a theme, click **Download ZIP**, upload it to **Overleaf**, set **Menu → Compiler → LuaLaTeX**, then **Recompile**.

## What You Edit

- `poster-….tex` — your poster: `\PosterSetup{title = …, authors = …}`, then your content. `\section{…}`, `theorem`, `definition`, `lemma`, `proof` … paste straight from a paper.
- `figures/` — your images.

Leave the engine alone: `postertheme.sty` (the whole look), `latexmkrc`, `fonts/`.
